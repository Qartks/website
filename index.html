Real Estate

Did you know that if you don’t have an American social security number, you are required to provide additional documentation when looking for a property to move into?

Thanks to our extensive local real estate network, we help you move into your new property with a seamless process that means no hassles for you.

Whether Rentals, sales or investments, with us, you get a clear and transparent understanding of the process and fees from the onset as well as a choice of the right type of property for you. We even provide you with video conferencing for those unable to be onsite.

Our Services Include:

  • Movers, Organizers & Storage

  • Repairs, Assemblies & Installations.

  • Setup & Transfer of Accounts: Inevitable with every move comes a period of transition where existing accounts from previous addresses need to be transferred, and new ones set up. A list of examples of how we can help to lighten your work load:

  • TV, Internet & Phone: (e.g. relocating/returning/issuing hardware; coordinating installations; transferring account information; bill pay)

  • Updating State Issued Documents to Reflect Change of Address: (e.g. licenses and registrations)

  • Facilitation of Issuance of Parking Permits

  • Changes of Address and Mail Forwarding with USPS

  • Renter’s Insurance

  • Staff member on site for any or all of the process

Medical & Wellness

Studying in a new city can be an exciting time in our lives and in the midst of that excitement we sometimes forget to prepare ourselves for medical support or emergencies. For example, what do you do if you’ve had an accident? Who do you go to if you have a dental emergency? How can you organise for a doctor to come and see you?

Whether it is finding the right medical professional, coordinating your appointments, accompanying you to the appointments or procedures or even in-home care, we ensure that your have a trusted advisor when it comes to your health and wellbeing throughout your stay here as a student, and beyond.

Our Services Include:

  • Finding you the right- health care professionals, Personal trainers,

  • Meal preparation & delivery services,

  • Nutritionists/dieticians and,

  • Other alternative health care providers.

Legal

A recent study of university students in the US showed that more than 80% of students had little (to no) knowledge of the legal implications to things like basic traffic violations, real estate disputes and other matters that require legal documentation.

Many of our past clients have had trouble with anything from eviction disputes to automobile and traffic infractions (such as duis, parking tickets, moving violations etc).

BCCG has the expertise and experience to provide advice regarding any such legal matters. We also accompany clients to any legal consultations and/or court proceedings and in extreme cases, support our clients in cases of Misdemeanor and Felony criminal matters.

Financial

Our years of experience has shown us that financial matters can be complicated to manage for students, even if you are studying accounting or finance!

This is why we help our clients with all matters that deal with finance to take the stress off you so that you can focus on your studies.

  • Bank account initiation and management.

  • Purchases on our card or a designated us card or account to avoid foreign card blocks or theft.

Insurance & Safety Services

“Safety does not come from being lucky. It comes from being prepared”

There have been countless times over the years when we have seen students undergo unnecessary distress in difficult situations, simply because they were just not prepared. This is why we do everything possible to ensure that this does not happen to our clients.

For example, in the event of an accident or emergency, BCCG has the ability to help students with literally everything from filing an accident report to personally transporting your vehicle to be serviced and dropped back off to you when its ready.
